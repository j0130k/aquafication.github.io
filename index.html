Sea turtles are ancient mariners present in all oceans especially in earth's coldest oceans. There are 7 types of sea turtles that have existed since the time of the dinosaurs. All these 7 types of turtles are among the oldest, well adapted and most primitive creatures, having evolved 120 millions of years ago. This reason named them as the majestical and awe-inspiring underwater creatures.
        
        
However, all the good days must come to an end. In this current decade, all 7 sea turtles species are threatened with extinction. Sea turtles are now listed in the Red List Categories. For instance, the Leatherback turtle is listed as a vulnerable species, the Green turtle on the other hand is listed as an endangered species while the Hawksbill turtle is listed as a critically endangered species. All this happened in a blink of an eye due to uncontrolled human activities. In particular, fishing activity is a major threat to the sea turtle as most of the turtles are accidentally caught in the fishing nets. Besides, illegal harvesting also contributes as a risk to the turtles population because this activity reduces the survival rate of the turtles. Not to forget, ocean pollution is also a concern in this situation. For example, disposals of plastic into the sea accidentally chokes a turtle when the turtle mistakenly consumes it thinking that the plastic as a jellyfish.
Addressing this severe issue, several authorities and policies have played a significant role to overcome this problem. For instance, many volunteers have helped to move sea turtles eggs to turtle hatchling nurseries due to nearby oil spillage at the beach. Policies also have been established to prohibit trafficking in turtle products, to reduce bycatch and protect turtles habitat. Although the authorities and policies are playing their role, it still takes two to tango. This means we should not be dependent on the authorities to overcome this issue by themselves. Instead, we should also lend a helping hand by playing our role as a concerned citizen towards our surroundings. For example, we as students have taken our initiatives by creating an informational website including a fun game about sea turtles to promote awareness among our society thus stopping the decline of sea turtles in planet earth. If such intervention is done by all individuals like us, not only turtles, but the survival rate of other endangered species will also be ensured.
