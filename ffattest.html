FUN FACTS ABOUT TURTLES

FACT 1

Temperature determines the gender of most turtles species. For example, if a turtle's eggs are incubated below 81.86 Fahrenheit, the turtle hatchlings will be a male. However, if a turtle's eggs are incubated above 87.8 Fahrenheit, the turtle hatchlings will be a female.

FACT 2

Turtles lose their first "baby tooth" within an hour. Every turtle hatchlings have only one tooth which is called the egg tooth on their beak. This small and sharp tooth helps them to cut through and hatch out their egg's shell. Once the turtle hatchlings are free from their shell, their egg tooth falls off within the hour.

FACT 3

Turtles are among the oldest animals around. As pets, some turtle species can live from 10 to 150+ years. For instance, the Flatback turtle or also known as Natator depressus has an average life span of 80 or more years.

FACT 4

Turtles can hold their breath for approximately 5 hours underwater. To fulfill this, they slow down their heart rate up to 9 minutes in between heartbeats to retain more oxygen in their lungs.

FACT 5

Sea turtles do "cry" sometimes. This is not because they are sad, instead it is to excrete excess salt in their body through their eyes. As reptile's kidneys are unable to remove large amount of salt through urine, thus turtles have special glands located in the corner of each eye to remove the excessive salt in their body.
